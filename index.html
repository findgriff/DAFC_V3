Who We Are

A club built on community, not just results

Darley Abbey FC was established in 2025 in the heart of Derby. We're not just about what happens on matchday — we're about the bonds formed at training, the families who come to watch, and the role football plays in this part of the city.

We believe sport has the power to shape character. Whether you're a player, a volunteer, or simply someone who loves the game, Darley Abbey FC is your club.

What We Stand For

Built on three pillars

Football

Quality football played with pride. We develop players at every level and believe in the beautiful game as a vehicle for personal growth.

Community

Deeply rooted in Darley Abbey and Derby. Our club exists to serve the local area and create lasting connections between people.

Development

Building champions on and off the pitch. We invest in our players as people first, footballers second — always focused on long-term growth.
