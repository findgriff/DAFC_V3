Brendon Tuckwood named Man of the Match

Darley Abbey FC · 22 February 2026 · 2 min read

Brendon Tuckwood is Darley Abbey FC's Man of the Match from Sunday's league fixture against Derby Athletic.

In a difficult afternoon, Brendon stood up with character and composure, showing quality in possession and leadership through key moments of the game.

He also stepped up for the penalty in the second half, taking responsibility at a critical point when DAFC were pushing to get back into the contest.

"Big character. Big performance. Fully deserved Man of the Match recognition."

Congratulations, Brendon. Keep setting the standard.
