About Darley Abbey FC

A fresh chapter in Derby's grassroots football story — rooted in community from day one.

Our History

Where it all began

Darley Abbey FC was established in 2025 in one of Derby's most characterful villages — a place with a rich heritage and a tight-knit community spirit that runs right through the heart of the club.

The club was founded with a clear purpose: to bring people together through football in Darley Abbey and the wider Derby area. Not just another team, but a community — one that believes sport is about far more than what happens on the pitch.

From day one, the focus has been on doing things the right way. Developing players. Supporting families. Giving people a reason to show up, week in, week out, for something they care about.

We're just getting started — and we're building something that will last.
